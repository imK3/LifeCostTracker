# Market Research

This folder contains market research and competitor analysis for the LifeCostTracker project.

## Files
- `market-analysis.md`: Market research and competitor analysis

## Summary
- **Market Overview**: Global personal finance software market projected to reach $1.5B by 2027 (CAGR 8.2%)
- **Key Competitors**: Mint, YNAB, PocketGuard, Truebill, Copilot
- **Target Users**: 
  - Credit-Conscious Millennial (28-38)
  - Aspirational Buyer (24-45)
- **Unique Value Proposition**: 
  - Daily Cost Perspective
  - Credit Life First
  - Wishlist Forecasting
  - Subscription + Installment Hub
